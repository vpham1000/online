Nightlab
May 10, Fri 6-8pm
Calling all adults! Come to Nightlab for an enjoyable night full of learning, games, food, and booze. Go to all MOST exhibits while stuffing your face with Peppino's pizza and special drinks from the York. Try virtual reality gaming or play classic games such as Read the Story.
Sensory Friendly
May 18, Sat 5-7pm
Sensory Friendly Time is when the noise, flashing lights, and air compressors get turned off so people with sensory processing challengers can come and have fun in the Museum. We have sensory friendly activities and kits for all who come to enjoy. The MOST wants everyone to get a chance to experience everything that it has to offer without any interruptions.
Be the Pilot
May 25, Sat 12-4pm
The motto for this event is "Learn, Engage, & Explore". Visitors will get to be scientists for the day. There will be hands-on learning with experiments where visitors will get to follow a real scientist's activities. This event is all about pilots and the science of aviation. This is a perfect learning experience!
